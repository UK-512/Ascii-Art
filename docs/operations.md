# Operationals

## Requirements

- Modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox)
- Secure context required:
  - `https://` OR `localhost`
- Camera permission required

---

## Usage Tips

### Performance Optimization

For low-end or mobile devices:

- Increase font size
- Reduce detail level
- Use black & white mode

---

## Known Constraints

- Performance depends on device CPU/GPU
- Browser limitations may affect camera resolution
